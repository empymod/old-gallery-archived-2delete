Magnetic fields and magnetic permeability¶

The main implementation of emg3d obtains the electric field due to an electric source. However, emg3d can also be used to model a magnetic source, obtain the magnetic field, or any combination thereof (\(ee\), \(em\), \(me\), \(mm\)).
